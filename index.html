Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best quality service to all our clients within the acceptable turnaround time by being committed to continuous review and improvement of our operations within a culture of customer focus, quality assurance, integration of information technology, HR development, and environmental responsibility. PQP’s purpose is to deliver excellence in trading to our stakeholders, associates, customers, vendors, investors, and the larger community, which we believe, will guarantee our own long-term prosperity.

ABOUT

KNOW US

PQP is an advanced, multifaceted, global commodities trading company that believes in adding value to its customer's businesses. Our core capabilities lie in the fast-moving discipline of global commodity trading, merging trading knowledge with impeccable logistics support for the product, ensuring excellent quality, and achieving impeccable perfection in delivering our promise.

We directly source quality agricultural products of international standards from farmers in India and supply them to the end customer. With tested and proven supply chain capabilities and logistics, we have always been dedicated to delivering outstanding products and services to our customers. We have state-of-the-art facilities, excellent practices, and procedures in place - that have helped us to protect the quality of our world-class products across each of our lines of business.

PQP’s International trading arm focuses mainly on agricultural commodities from sourcing to managing complete supply chain arrangements for Spices, Sugar, Rice, Maize, Wheat, Barley Millet, etc. We are experts in supplying to all industries of the market ensuring quality products as per their requirements and speciﬁcations.

We are strategically located in the northern heartland of Indian agricultural production from where we empower sourcing and manage our supply chain. We believe that agriculture is the backbone of many developing countries, fuels societies, and brings change. By bridging the supply chain from farmers to consumers, PQP aims to be recognized as a distinguished player of socio-economic signiﬁcance in the world. We strive to ensure that high-quality products that inﬂuence lives in a positive manner are supplied. It is essential that these products become a part of the end-users everyday life.
